Anguish
https://youtu.be/jUP7RMcR3gM
Directed by Jiin Jung Performed by Paul Wegener Sound design by Naico Anguish is a great mental suffering or physical pain. The number 108 means 108 anguishes in Buddhism. 108 bows is the penance of Buddhists, which is 108 times to perform the process of kneeling, lying down, and rising again. This action removing anguish one by one. This work is metaphorically speaking of anguish we face in life and the corresponding chaos.
